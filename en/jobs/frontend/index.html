Front End Expert

MonkeyPatch IT expertise company is looking for Front-End consultants. Are you a technophile and like technical challenges? Would you like to join a dynamic team passionate about IT?

Position and missions

As a Front-End Expert, you will play a real pivotal role between Design (UX/UI) and Back-End Development. You will have as missions:

  • Contribute to front-end development
  • Propose new features and improvements
  • Provide technical advice and expertise
  • To be a force of proposal on performance and architecture
  • Ensure a permanent monitoring around the uses, technologies in the field of web & mobile
  • And (depending on mission and experience) ensure the technical lead of the project

Within our team, you will also be required to regularly participate in technical events to exchange new technical orientations and share your know-how.

Required profile

We are looking for passionate developers who like to develop web applications. With a Bachelor’s degree and more than 5 years of experience, HTML5, CSS3 and responsive design have no secrets for you. You know how to use all the possibilities of native JavaScript and the main associated frameworks:

  • JavaScript, EmacScript 6+, TypeScript
  • HTML5 / CSS 3
  • At least one recent js framework (Angular, React, Vue.js, …)
  • NodeJS tools

More

The mastery of REST web services and a back-end environment is a plus.

  • Compensation: Attractive
  • Type of contract: permanent contract
