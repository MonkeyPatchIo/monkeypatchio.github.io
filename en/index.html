Who are we?

MonkeyPatch is a Toulouse-based IT consulting and implementation company created in March 2016. It covers activities:

  • of IT expertise,
  • architecture consulting or IT governance,
  • for the development of software or Web services (SaaS, Cloud, BigData, etc.),
  • for the development of mobile applications.

MonkeyPatch is surrounded only by IT experts in the Web, Mobile and Big Data businesses.

Our teams are made up exclusively of people with a pronounced taste for computers and wishing to stay in technical positions to be as close as possible to the code. All have more than 5 years of experience and significant references in Web, Mobile or Big Data. To maintain and develop their level of expertise, they benefit from dedicated time (17 days/year) to experiment with technologies and pool their know-how.
